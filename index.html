My Recipies

If you want to improve your cooking skills but don’t know where to start, we’ve got plenty of dishes to choose from. Our collection includes a variety of different recipes both savoury and sweet.

Roast potatoes

Roast potatoes, which you can see being made in the video above, are served as part of a classic roast dinner spread. Perfecting the skill of achieving soft on the inside, crisp on the outside potatoes is much easier than you may think with our step-by-step video guide.

Victoria sandwich

Everyone loves a classic Victoria sponge, especially when it's homemade. This is definitely one of the first recipes everyone should know how to cook when first starting out. You'll feel like a pro in the kitchen once you've made this delightful treat.

Cottage pie

Smothered with a creamy mash topping and packed full of tender meat and gravy, cottage pie is another classic dish to master. We're rating this dish as a medium skill level just as you have two things to watch over and make at the same time, but if you've got multitasking down to a fine art, this recipe is going to be an easy one for you.
